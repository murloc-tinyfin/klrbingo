Kirby live radio bingo

Rules:

  1. You have to gain 1000 XP on top of the XP you already have on the leaderboard for each card that contains that space. When you achieve the 300 XP you must announce it to the chat.
  2. For the specific song category, remixes are allowed, but it has to be an individual remix, not a medley.
  3. Matching theme means the theme playing is the theme that's supposed to be playing in the gameplay corner
  4. For the space of all 4 seasons themes, the 4 themes don't have to be in a row
  5. Songs from remakes of games count for the original game, so a Kirby's Adventure track counts for Kirby Nightmare In Dream Land and vice versa.
  6. If there are are any questions or concerns let us know. Suggestions for bingo spaces are also accepted.
  7. Most importantly, have fun!
